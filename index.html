Hi All,

With the spirit and enthusiasm to better the community we live in and share with millions of people, our mission is to create sustainable change for the marginalized and underprivileged sections of society. As part of our community service, awareness programs about environmental sustainability and renewable energy is an urgent part of our CSR endeavours.

By working on the ground principles of humanity, ethics, and integrity, our initiatives and efforts strive to empower the needy in the best possible way that we can. As we prioritize environmental sustainability, we are consciously moving towards transforming all lives with energy independence. The objective is to conserve our ecosystem by adopting projects that drive renewable energy and by association, the larger environment.

I take this opportunity to thank my entire team and our partners who have been the torch-bearers in our pursuit of driving happiness and helping the community. The mission is to bring a 'Smile' and spread it universally. I thank our volunteers for the countless hours they've so selflessly spent in support of this noble cause.

We stay true to our commitment to building a more inclusive and sustainable world that keeps getting stronger as we synthesize our efforts towards a greener, safer, and better tomorrow for everyone.

The spirit of NeoSmile Foundation is to create equitable, inclusive & sustainable change that will positively impact 10 million lives across Rural India by 2025.

We believe collaboration is the key to sustainable change and seek active partners and volunteers to support our ongoing CSR initiatives around the year.

Environment Day
Celebrations

June 5

100+ NeoSmile Foundation volunteers reached out to the corners of Matunga, Kandivali, and Badlapur areas in Mumbai to plant 200+ varieties of flowering and fruit plants, and vegetable crops.

More than 160 special children from 'The Children Aid Society' in Matunga participated in this drive to create a 'Butterfly Garden' which is now adorned with beautiful flowers and fruit plants.

90+ Hours volunteered | 250+ Saplings planted

Renewable Energy
Drive in Ratnagiri

May 14

NeoSmile Foundation, under its Renewable Energy initiative, has successfully installed solar panels in the Ratnagiri district on May 14th.

Our teams conducted workshops for 500 students in a municipal school to spread awareness about global warming & its impact on the environment.

100+ Hours volunteered | 1500+ Lives touched

Clean Energy
Campaign in Akola

June 19

25+ street lights and 10 & 5 KVA solar panels were installed in Kasturi village of Doanwada and Katel Dham, in Akola District and a message to embrace renewable energy was spread amongst the local community.The project aims to reduce 7 metric tons of Co2 emission per year.

100+ Hours volunteered | 210000 Lives touched
